About Us

Jebal Developments. A leading real-estate corporate in New Cairo was founded in 2010 by Civil Engineer Ahmed Attia Aletr, former teaching and research assistant at the GUC and current founder and CEO of Jebal Co. Engineer Aletr years of experience in research in the field of civil construction and foundation has inspired him to establish the first real estate company in Tagmou that has both business and national-serving vision; unlike other profit-seeking companies, Jebal is dedicated to resolving the housing crisis that has resulted from our country's current economical unstability.

MISSION

To provide housing properties that allow our customers to have residency in the elite, new Capital of Cairo, New Cairo, and also to enable investors the opportunity to protect and increase the value of their assets.

Vision

To be a brilliant real-estate corporate in Egypt
